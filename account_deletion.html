Account Deletion Request

Flash AI

To request deletion of your Flash AI account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

📧 How to Request Account Deletion

Step 1: Send an email to:

abhishek@flashai.co.in

Step 2: Email Subject:

"Account Deletion Request - Flash AI"

Step 3: Include in your email:

• Your registered email address (Google account or phone number used for login)
• Your shop name (if applicable)
• Reason for deletion (optional)

Send Deletion Request

⏱️ Processing Time

We will process your account deletion request and permanently remove all associated data within 7 business days.

⚠️ Important Information

  • Permanent Action: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Data Removed: All jewelry images, AI-generated photoshoots, product catalogs, and shop information will be deleted.
  • Website Removed: Your e-commerce website will be taken offline.
  • Coins/Credits: Any remaining coins or credits will be forfeited.
  • No Refunds: Purchases made prior to deletion are non-refundable.

📋 What Data Will Be Deleted?

Personal Information

Name, email address, phone number, and profile details

Business Information

Shop name, address, logo, Instagram handle, and custom domain

Images & Media

All uploaded jewelry photos and AI-generated images

E-commerce Data

Product catalog, collections, categories, and website customizations

Transaction History

Coin purchase history, coin balance, and usage records

Account Data

Authentication tokens, app preferences, and settings

Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Connected Data

If you connected Flash AI with your Facebook Page or Instagram Business Account for marketing automation, we will delete all associated Meta data including:

• Facebook access tokens
• Instagram access tokens
• Connected Facebook Page IDs
• Connected Instagram Business Account IDs
• Marketing automation permissions granted through Meta APIs

🔗 Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Data Removal

If you connected Flash AI with Facebook or Instagram, you can also remove Flash AI's access directly from your Meta account settings.

Remove App Access from Facebook

You may revoke Flash AI access anytime by visiting:

https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=applications

What happens after removal?

Once access is revoked or your account is deleted, Flash AI will no longer have access to your Facebook Page or Instagram Business Account data.

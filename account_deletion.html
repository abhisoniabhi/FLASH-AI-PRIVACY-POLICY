To request deletion of your Flash AI account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

📧 How to Request Account Deletion

Step 1: Send an email to:

abhishek@flashai.co.in

Step 2: Email Subject:

"Account Deletion Request - Flash AI"

Step 3: Include in your email:

• Your registered email address (Google account or phone number used for login)
• Your shop name (if applicable)
• Reason for deletion (optional)

Send Deletion Request

⏱️ Processing Time

We will process your account deletion request and permanently remove all associated data within 7 business days of receiving your email. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

⚠️ Important Information

  • Permanent Action: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Data Removed: All your jewelry images, AI-generated photoshoots, product catalogs, and shop information will be permanently deleted.
  • Website Removed: Your e-commerce website will be taken offline immediately.
  • Coins/Credits: Any remaining coins or credits will be forfeited and cannot be refunded.
  • No Refunds: Purchases made prior to deletion are non-refundable.
  • New Account: You can create a new account anytime, but previous data cannot be recovered.

📋 What Data Will Be Deleted?

Personal Information

Name, email address, phone number, and profile details

Business Information

Shop name, address, logo, Instagram handle, and custom domain

Images & Media

All uploaded jewelry photos, AI-generated images, product images, and collection banners

E-commerce Data

Product catalog, collections, categories, and website customizations

Transaction History

Coin purchase history, coin balance, and usage records

Account Data

Authentication tokens, app preferences, and settings

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No, account deletion is permanent. All data is permanently removed from our servers.

How long does the deletion process take?

We process deletion requests within 7 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email once complete.

What happens to my website?

Your e-commerce website will be taken offline immediately upon account deletion.

Will I get a refund for unused coins?

No, unused coins are forfeited upon account deletion and are non-refundable.
